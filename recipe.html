Making Baked Feta and Tomato Pasta

Ingredients!

Tomatoes

Feta

Garlic

Olive Oil

Salt N Peppa

Red pepper flakes

Basil

Steps to Make the Dish:

- gather ingredients!!!

- boil and salt water :)

- add pasta woot

- while pasta is cooking, in a pan, put tomatos, feta, garlic, olive oil, salt, pepper, red pepper flakes, and mix together!

- bake pan until feta is golden, or when tomatoes are bursting

- mix together baked dish and cooked pasta to get final, yummy result !!!

The reason why this dish is so fun and fresh to make and eat is because it is simple yet classy. It of course is pasta, the greatest food ever, and it's an easy recipe to make, and to change to fit your taste. Whether it be a dish for one, or dinner for friends, it's the perfect meal. Make this dish and make your home a happier, yummier, greater place to be!
